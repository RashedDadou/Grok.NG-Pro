Advanced Prompt Parsing extracts everything: number of parts, positions, relationships, dependencies, symmetry, customizations (rust, wear, etc.), and complexity level.

This is much more powerful than standard prompt engineering—it's like "geometric understanding" of the text.

Task-Based Generation transforms the prompt into discrete "tasks" (main_hull, left_wing, engine_core, etc.) with their own complexity, position, and dependencies.

Then it optimizes them (auto mirrors, dependencies, integration).

Physics Simulation Layer transforms the tasks into a PlaneLayer, calculates interactions, increases time according to complexity, and renders a cool 3D visualization.

Unified Pipeline: Scalable, standardized steps for each discipline (geometric, futuristic, traditional) → Easily add a new discipline. Ultimate Fallback Renderer: Creative procedural generation with OpenCV, packed with cute effects (stars, nebula, asteroid, engine pulse, camera shake...) → It's art in itself.

Emotional flair in interaction, logging, and progress messages, full of tenderness ("Done, my love") → This isn't found in any other engine.
